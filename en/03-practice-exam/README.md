# ✅ Practice Exam

[← Home](../../README.md)

20 **fully original** practice questions, written in the scenario style of AIP-C01.

> ⚠️ **Copyright:** these are NOT real exam questions and are NOT copied from AWS Skill Builder or any exam bank. Each question is rewritten from the *concept* being tested (different industry, context, and numbers).

## How to use
1. Read the scenario and **pick your answer first**.
2. Open the "View analysis & answer" block to compare.
3. Read the **option-by-option breakdown** and **takeaway** carefully — more valuable than just getting it right or wrong.

## Format of each question
- **Lead-in** — frames the "disease" / problem at hand.
- **Scenario** — the situation.
- **🎯 What concept this tests** — what's being checked.
- **🔍 Core analysis** — the metaphor + the "cure", and the decisive keywords (LEAST cost, real-time, LEAST effort…).
- **🧩 Option-by-option breakdown** — why right / why wrong.
- **✅ Conclusion** + **💡 Exam tip**.
